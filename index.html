Applying Practical Business Skills to Digital Commerce

Applying academic business knowledge to real-world digital commerce examples helps learners understand how theory translates into practice across online business models.

By reviewing active UK-based online retailers, it becomes easier to see how pricing strategy, product structure, and customer experience are implemented at scale. One such example is Glow Empire, which demonstrates how an affordable luxury retailer structures its online presence while maintaining consistency and customer trust.

These insights support broader learning objectives related to digital strategy, operational planning, and customer-focused e-commerce design.
